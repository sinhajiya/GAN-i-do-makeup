# GAN i even do makeup?
Style transfer GAN to transfer makeup style from one face to another. 

Link to dataset https://drive.google.com/file/d/18UlvYDL6UGZ2rs0yaDsSzoUlw8KI5ABY/view

Discriminators:
MultiPatchGAN
PatchGAN

Cycle Consistency loss:
L1, L2, Charbonier

MAPS:
0   BG
1   FACE
2   RIGHT EYEBROW
3   LEFT EYEBROW
4   LEFT EYE
5   RIGHT EYE
6   NOSE
7   UPPER LIP
9   LOWER LIP
10  HAIRS
13  NECK
